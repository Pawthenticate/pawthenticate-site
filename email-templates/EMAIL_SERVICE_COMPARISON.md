## 💡 Pro Tips

### 1. Use Multiple Services

**Don't limit yourself to one!**

Example setup:
```
Marketing:        MailerLite (newsletters, launches)
Transactional:    Mailgun (signups, notifications)
Cold Outreach:    Lemlist or Reply.io
```

### 2. Start Free, Scale Smart

**Launch strategy:**
1. Start with MailerLite (free, full features)
2. Build your list to 1,000 subscribers
3. Evaluate if you need more advanced features
4. Upgrade or switch only when necessary

### 3. Consider Deliverability

**All services have good deliverability IF you:**
- Verify your domain (SPF, DKIM)
- Warm up your sending (start small)
- Use double opt-in
- Clean your list regularly
- Avoid spam trigger words

### 4. Test Before Committing

**Most services offer:**
- Free trials (SendGrid, Mailgun)
- Free tiers (MailerLite, Mailchimp)
- Money-back guarantees

**Test for 30 days before choosing!**

## 🚀 Getting Started

### For MailerLite (Recommended)

1. Sign up at [mailerlite.com](https://www.mailerlite.com)
2. Read our complete guide: [`MAILERLITE_GUIDE.md`](MAILERLITE_GUIDE.md)
3. Create waitlist group
4. Build embedded form
5. Add to your website
6. Start collecting subscribers!

**Time to set up:** 15-30 minutes

---

### For Mailgun (Transactional)

1. Sign up at [mailgun.com](https://www.mailgun.com)
2. Verify your domain
3. Get SMTP credentials
4. Configure in `.env.local`
5. Test with Supabase Auth

**Time to set up:** 1-2 hours (domain verification)

---

### For SendGrid (Alternative)

1. Sign up at [sendgrid.com](https://sendgrid.com)
2. Create API key
3. Verify sender domain
4. Add SMTP settings
5. Import email templates

**Time to set up:** 1-2 hours

## ❓ FAQs

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I switch services later?</strong></summary>

Yes! Most services let you:
- Export subscriber lists (CSV)
- Export email templates
- Switch at any time

**Switching is easy.** Only thing you lose is historical analytics.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Do I need multiple services?</strong></summary>

**For startups:** No, MailerLite alone is enough

**For scale:** Yes, separate marketing (MailerLite) from transactional (Mailgun/SendGrid)
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Which service has best deliverability?</strong></summary>

**All major services have similar deliverability (98%+) IF:**
- You verify your domain
- Use double opt-in
- Clean your list
- Follow best practices

**Mailgun & SendGrid** have slightly better reputation for transactional emails.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I use Gmail for my startup?</strong></summary>

**No!** Gmail limits:
- 500 emails/day
- High spam risk
- No tracking
- Unprofessional

Use a real email service from day 1.
</details>
